Getting started with Template.apex

Installation

Template.apex has a dependency on R.apex and Script.apex.

Include these first.

Then, we will include Template.apex.

Include Template.cls, and TemplateTest.cls(optional) into your Org, and you are ready to go.

If you want to use Func Package, please include TemplatePackage.cls. You can check more on R.apex.
